Mailing Lists

We have started a google group for discussing JBoost. You can ask questions and get answers from other users or developers by joining this group. Even if you don't have any problems, please feel free to join the group and make comments or suggestions.

Click here to join: http://groups.google.com/group/jboost/subscribe

Once you have joined the group, you can send your questions/comments to

jboost-at-googlegroups-dot-com

* This google group does not carry over the subscriptions from the sourceforge mailing lists. Although you have subscribed with the sourceforge mailing lists, you will need to subscribe with the google group again. Please follow the link above to join the google group.
